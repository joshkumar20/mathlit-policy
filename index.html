Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 6, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how MathLit ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the MathLit Android application. By using MathLit, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

a) Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

  • Your Google display name
  • Your Google profile picture URL
  • Your Google account email address
  • A unique Firebase User ID (UID) assigned to your account

b) Game & Progress Data

  • Game sessions: mode, operation, score, correct/wrong answers, accuracy, duration
  • Daily challenge completion and results
  • XP points, level, current streak, and longest streak
  • Total games played, total score, and highest score

c) Device & Usage Data (via Google AdMob)

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data for ad personalization

d) Locally Stored Data

  • Streak and last-played date (stored in device SharedPreferences)
  • Game history (stored in local Room database on your device)
  • Daily challenge completion status (stored locally)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save and display your game progress, streaks, and leaderboard rankings
  • To generate your Daily Challenge and track completion
  • To calculate XP, levels, and achievements
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To send in-app notifications about challenges, streaks, or new content

3. Data Storage & Security

Your account and game data is stored on our secure backend server hosted on Railway (railway.app). Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication. All API communication is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Some data (game history, streak, daily challenge status) is also stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

We implement industry-standard security practices, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Children's Privacy

MathLit is designed for general audiences and is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 beyond what is necessary to provide the app's core features.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at the email below and we will promptly delete the information.

6. Leaderboard & Public Data

Your display name, level, and score may be visible to other users on the in-app leaderboard. No other personal information (such as your email address) is shared publicly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and game data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out via your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. Permissions Used by the App

  • INTERNET — Required to connect to our backend API, Firebase, and load ads
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Used to check connectivity before making API calls
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Used to reschedule daily reminder notifications after device restart

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
